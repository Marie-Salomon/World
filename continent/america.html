The Americas is a continent in the Western Hemisphere. It extends from the Arctic Ocean in the north, to Cape Horn in the Drake Passage in the south, at the confluence of the Atlantic and Pacific oceans that delimit it to the east and west, respectively. With more than 42 million km2, the Americas is the second largest continent on the planet1, covering 8.3% of the total land area and 28.2% of the world's land surface. In addition, America is home to about 13.3% of the world's population,with more than a billion people.
