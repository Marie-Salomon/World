Asia is one of the continents or part of the Eurasia or Afro-Eurasia supercontinents of the Earth. With 43,810,582 km2 of land and 4.3 billion inhabitants, Asia is the largest continent (8.6% of the total land area or 29.4% of the world's land surface) and the most populous (about 60% of the world's population). Asia is more of a cultural concept than a homogeneous physical entity. This continent is home to the highest peak in the world, Everest, which rises to 8,849 meters, and the world's tallest tower, Burj Khalifa, in Dubai,
